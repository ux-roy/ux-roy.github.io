Title:
My Low Tide

Time:
2015

Medium:
Acrylic on canvas

Dimension:
4' × 2'5"

Depiction:
A golden figure curls inward in a quiet moment of sadness. The heavy background reflects a difficult phase, but the warm color shows hope. It’s a pause, a breath, a gathering of strength. It reminds us that low moments are only pauses, not the end.
