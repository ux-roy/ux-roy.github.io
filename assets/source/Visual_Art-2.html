Title:
The Golden Cage

Time:
2008

Medium:
Acrylic on canvas

Dimension:
6'5" Ã— 5'

Depiction:
A quiet path glows in soft sunlight, framed by tall trees and distant houses. It feels warm and inviting, yet a bright gate blocks the way. The contrast creates a feeling of beauty just out of reach. The scene reflects how life sometimes asks us to wait, holding onto a quiet hope that the gate will one day open.
