Title:
Sei Nauka Ghate

Time:
2007

Medium:
Watercolor on paper

Dimension:
2'5" × 2'25"

Depiction:
A quiet river holds a small world of resting boats, each one carrying stories of places they’ve been and places they’re yet to go. The calm water feels like a gentle pause after a long day — a reminder that even journeys need moments of stillness. This painting invites the viewer to slow down, breathe, and find peace in the simple act of waiting.
